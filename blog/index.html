A ROMANTIQUE TOUR AT HACIENDA ISABELLA

By: Jae-Mar Medina Arenque | June 1, 2017

Our company planned to take a short vacation to have our team building. We chose Hacienda Isabella at Indang Cavite. It is not a so near place to take. We stayed for 2 days and 1 night to unwind and relax. It's a perfect getaway for classic and vintage-type of people who are appreciative of old and antique things.

Here's are some of the best spot of the Hacienda Isabella

Teleserye ambiance

This is the main entrance of the Hacienda Isabella. A teleserye type entrance, with falling petals and leaves, cinematic entourage, and glances of the sun from the trees. All i can ask is a classy background music.

Antique furnitures everywhere

What I also like about this place is it feels like I am in a Philippine history movie scene. Which in fact this hacienda is a favorite location of Philippine movies and teleserye like Till I Met You starred by Regine Velasques and Robin Padilla, Petrang Kabayo Vice Ganda’s blockbuster movie, Beautiful Affair a reunion teleserye of Bea Alonzo and John Lloyd Cruz aired at ABS-CBN Primetime Bida and Now & Forever – starred by Sheryl Cruz and Zoren Legaspi aired at GMA 7.

My favorite spot of the house. We played Jengga here when we arrived.

Vintage

Hacienda Isabela is owned by Kuh Ledesma who is a popular singer but also a frustrated Architect so this place was actually built for the purpose of her designing it. It is famous as an event place and team buildings even retreats. It's not your ordinary event place because of the historical feel it gives. It resembles to Pinto Art Museum's vibe because of the art works, huge paintings, vintage decors and white walls. The place is bombarded with antiques and her collections of old cameras, furniture and suitcases which she got from flea markets.

Hacienda Isabella, named after Kuh’s daughter , is located in Indang, Cavite. Kuh’s dream was only to build a small farmhouse surrounded by fruit-bearing trees.
This are some of the paintings and arrangements inside the house.

State of the art

Everywhere you put my eyes you'll see state of the arts arrangements. That's why this venue is very famous for wedding and debut venues.

Another thing is the food they serve. We eat in a very long table (can accomodate more than 20). At the morning they served fresh baked pandesal, pancakes, fried scrambled eggs and some fruits like pineapple and papaya.

Here's a look of some of their facilities including the long pool (8ft.).

We enjoyed every part of this hacienda, our travel time is so worth it. The environment was so relaxing. If you want peace of mind and escape from stress world visit it. I highly recommend it!
